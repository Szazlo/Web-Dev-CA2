UniRunner

Lore

You are a college student just trying to just pass your course. Struggling to attend lectures, you are trying to get a good grade. In this game you will be running late to lectures, and attempting to make it on time. You will be given time to run as the day begins slow and paces over time, and you will have to run from the lecturers chasing you to class.

How To Play

You are to navigate your player || around the campus (map) between buildings, using the arrow key on your keyboard. Your objective is to attend your lectures (collect || ), your goal is reach the end of year 4 by collecting 60 credits every year. Each lecture with give you 20 credits in 1st year and decrease by 5 credits every year there after. The lecturers || will chase you and increase speed over time. !!!Spoiler!!!:God mode can be enabled by pressing "G", meaning the game will continue after being caught.
